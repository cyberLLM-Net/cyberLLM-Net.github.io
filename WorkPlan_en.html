Working Plan

Objective 1: Analysis of datasets and AI models for network-based threat detection based on LLMs.

The purpose of this work package is to analyse the literature in search of data collections and state-of-the-art AI models for network-based threat detection. Data collections will be used for the development of specific LLMs based on network protocols that could improve performance of existing threat detection AI models. Additionally, new metrics will be formulated to conduct an evaluation based on absolute time. This package is directly related to the general objective GO1 and to the specific objectives SO1 and SO4.

Objective 2: Development of specific LLMs for network-based threat detection.

This work package constitutes the core of the proposed research project and is centred in the development of specific LLMs based on network traffic for cyber-threat detection to be later integrated on a SIEM. Two approaches will be considered: the adaptation of pre-trained text-based LLMs for the detection of threats based on network traffic and the development, from scratch, of specific LLMs based on network protocols. The tasks contained are linked with the general objective GO1 and the specific objectives SO2 and SO4.

Objective 3: Development of integration mechanisms with SIEM network-traffic based LLMs.

This work package is aimed at establishing mechanisms that allow integrating the results of the models developed in WP3 to enhance the efficiency, effectiveness, and precision of Security Information and Event Management systems. This WP is linked to the general objective GO2 and the specific objectives SO3 and SO4.
